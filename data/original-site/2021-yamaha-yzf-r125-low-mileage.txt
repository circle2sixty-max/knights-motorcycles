Description
This 2021 Yamaha YZF-R125 is a superb example of Yamaha's class-leading 125cc sports bike. It brings the full racetrack aesthetic of the legendary R range to the A1 licence category, featuring the same aggressive bodywork, dual headlights, and premium detailing as its bigger brothers.
The R125 is renowned for its focused riding position, sophisticated suspension with an inverted fork, and the rigid Deltabox frame that delivers sharp, precise handling. Whether you're a new rider looking for your first taste of sports bike performance, or an experienced enthusiast wanting a nimble and economical machine for weekend fun, this R125 delivers an engaging and authentic experience.
