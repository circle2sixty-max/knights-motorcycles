Description
This 2019 Yamaha MT125 ABS is a superb example of Yamaha's popular entry-level naked bike. It brings the bold, aggressive design language of the larger MT family to the 125cc class, creating a machine that looks as thrilling as it is to ride. The lightweight chassis and inverted front fork deliver sharp, responsive handling that inspires confidence in new riders and puts a smile on the face of any enthusiast.
With 11,200 miles on the clock, this MT125 is well-proven and ready for many more. The 15bhp liquid-cooled, four-stroke engine is smooth, refined, and perfectly suited to A1 licence requirements. The six-speed gearbox lets you make the most of the power, whether you're filtering through city traffic or enjoying a weekend B-road blast.
