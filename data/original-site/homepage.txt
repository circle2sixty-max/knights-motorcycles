About Us 🏍️
Welcome to Knights Motorcycles
Based in West Yorkshire,
Knights Motorcycles
is a trusted motorcycle dealer specializing in quality used bikes from 125cc to 1000cc. we've built our reputation through honest dealing and reliable service.
Our storage maintains a carefully selected stock of more than 30 motorcycles, covering various styles from learner-friendly 125cc bikes to powerful 1000cc machines. Whether you're looking for your first bike or upgrading to something more powerful, we can help you find the right motorcycle for your needs.
Based in Leeds,
Knights Motorcycles
focuses on providing well-maintained used motorcycles. Every bike in our storage undergoes a thorough pre-delivery inspection, including oil change, brake fluid and coolant check, ensuring it's ready for the road. 🇬🇧
Our Service Standards 🌟
At
Knights Motorcycles
, we believe proper preparation is essential before any motorcycle leaves our workshop. Our comprehensive pre-delivery service ensures each bike meets strict standards for safety and reliability. Here's our detailed preparation process:
Quality Assurance
HPI Checks
🔍: Before offering any motorcycle for sale, we conduct thorough history checks through HPI's database. This comprehensive verification process examines the bike's complete background, including any outstanding finance agreements, insurance write-off status, mileage discrepancies, or potential theft records. This crucial step ensures you can purchase with complete confidence, knowing the bike's legal and financial status is clear.
Warranty Coverage
🛡️: Every motorcycle comes with our standard 30-day warranty for your peace of mind. Extended warranties are available - please ask for full details during your visit.
Test Ride Guarantee
✅: Our professional riders thoroughly test each motorcycle to ensure everything works perfectly.
Pre-Delivery Service
Basic Inspection
🔧: Our experienced technicians perform a methodical inspection of all critical components to ensure optimal safety and performance. This includes:
A complete brake system evaluation, checking pad wear, disc condition, fluid levels, and proper operation of both front and rear brakes
Thorough chain and sprocket assessment, examining tension, wear patterns, and lubrication status
Detailed tire inspection covering tread depth, pressure levels, and overall condition including any signs of damage or aging
Comprehensive testing of all electrical systems including lights, indicators, horn, and dashboard functions
Assessment of steering head bearings, fork operation, and suspension components
Maintenance Service
🛠️: Every motorcycle undergoes essential maintenance procedures before delivery:
Complete oil change using appropriate grade engine oil to ensure optimal engine protection
Brake fluid level check and top-up using high-quality DOT4 fluid where needed
Thorough coolant system inspection with top-up of manufacturer-recommended coolant
Detailed safety inspection covering control cables, levers, and all mounting points
Verification of proper throttle operation and idle speed
Basic engine performance assessment including cold starting and running temperature, gear box and all systems in normal working condition
Customer Services 😊
At
Knights Motorcycles
, we aim to make motorcycle buying straightforward and hassle-free. We're happy to answer any questions and help you find a bike that suits your needs.
Our Services:
Part Exchange Available 🔄
: Looking to upgrade from your current ride? Whether you're stepping up from a middleweight to a litre bike, or switching from a sportbike to an adventure tourer, we welcome part-exchange deals. We'll give you a fair valuation based on market conditions and your bike's spec.
UK Delivery 🚚
: Can't collect in person? We can arrange delivery to your location within the UK. We understand that not everyone has the means to collect their motorcycle in person. That's why we offer a reliable delivery service, ensuring that your new bike arrives at your doorstep safely and promptly.
Instant Cash Purchase 💰
: Looking to sell your motorcycle? We buy bikes directly with immediate payment! Whether you need quick cash or simply want a hassle-free selling experience, we offer competitive on-the-spot payments via cash or same-day bank transfer. No waiting for buyers, no time-wasters, and no payment delays. Our experienced team will assess your bike's value and make you a fair offer based on current market conditions. From sports bikes to cruisers, we're interested in all makes and models in good condition.
Our Commitment To You 🤝
As a buyer from
Knights Motorcycles
, we believe in complete transparency and comprehensive support throughout your purchasing journey. Here's what you can expect when you choose to do business with us:
Buyer Support
Documentation Support 📄
We provide all essential paperwork to ensure a smooth and legal transfer of ownership. This includes:
A valid V5C registration document (logbook) that confirms you as the rightful owner
Any available service history documentation, though please note this varies by motorcycle - some bikes may have comprehensive records while others might have limited or no service history
Previous MOT history that can be verified online
Written receipt of purchase for your records
Viewing Experience 🔍
We structure our viewing process to ensure you can make an informed decision:
Flexible appointment scheduling to suit your availability, including evenings and weekends
Professional walk-around demonstration highlighting key features and any notable aspects
Detailed explanation of the bike's history and previous ownership
Opportunity to inspect the bike thoroughly in our well-lit workshop
Test start and systems check demonstration
Time to ask questions and discuss any concerns
No pressure or rush during the viewing process
Assistance with paperwork completion if you decide to purchase
