Description
This 2022 KTM Duke 125 is an excellent opportunity to own one of the most popular and highly-regarded 125cc motorcycles on the market. Known affectionately as "The Scalpel" for its sharp handling and precision, the Duke 125 offers a riding experience that feels far more substantial than its engine size suggests.
Finished in stealthy black, this example presents well and has covered just 7,000 miles. The 125cc single-cylinder engine is renowned for its punchy low-end torque, making it ideal for urban riding and confident progress on faster A-roads. The lightweight trellis frame, upside-down front forks, and radial brakes (features borrowed from KTM's larger capacity bikes) provide a level of handling that sets the Duke apart from conventional 125cc commuters.
Whether you are taking your first steps on an A1 licence, or simply want a stylish and capable runabout, this KTM delivers in spades.
