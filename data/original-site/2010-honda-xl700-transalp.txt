Description
This 2010 Honda XL700 Transalp is a fantastic example of one of the most respected adventure motorcycles ever built. The seller describes it as being in "great condition," and with 23,000 miles, this V-twin is just nicely run in. Honda’s 680cc engine is renowned for its smooth power delivery and legendary longevity, making this bike a trusted companion for years to come.
The Transalp's genius is its simplicity and versatility. The upright seating position offers excellent comfort and visibility in traffic, while the long-travel suspension and chain drive make it capable of handling rough roads and light trails with ease. The electric start fires up the motor instantly, and the six-speed gearbox is smooth and well-spaced for both town work and motorway cruising.
Presented in a striking white, this bike stands out from the crowd. Whether you are a seasoned adventure rider looking for a classic, or a commuter wanting something more capable and characterful than a standard street bike, this Transalp is an outstanding choice.
